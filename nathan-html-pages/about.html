JBE INDUSTRIES.

AIR CONDITIONING,
COMMERCIAL REFRIGERATION
AND ELECTRICAL.

Servicing Lake Macquarie, Newcastle, Central Coast and the Hunter region with over 20 years experience, the team at JBE Industries can assist you with all of your Air Conditioning, Commercial Refrigeration and Electrical needs. You can be assured that you will get the right products for your home or business at the right price with no hidden costs.

From quotes and installation; through to the service, maintenance and repairs; JBE Industries has the knowledge and expertise to provide quality customer service and workmanship, with clear communication throughout the entire process.

JBE Industries installs, repairs and maintains a wide variety of Air Conditioning, Commercial Refrigeration and Electrical systems. Whether it’s for domestic, commercial or Industrial, we provide a range of high quality Air Conditioning and Electrical products and solutions.
